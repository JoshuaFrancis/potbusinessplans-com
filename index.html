What's in the plan

Six sections that every Health Canada reviewer needs to see, done properly.

01

Executive Summary

A two-page overview that frames the business opportunity, ownership structure, licence category, and economic rationale before the reviewer opens the main document. Written to the specific expectations of the Health Canada pre-screening stage.

02

Facility and Security

Narrative description of the physical facility, access controls, CCTV coverage, vault specifications, and outdoor area management aligned to the Cannabis Regulations security requirements. Reviewed against the Health Canada Good Production Practices guide.

03

Standard Operating Procedures

SOP framework covering receiving, storage, production, quality control, waste destruction, record-keeping, and personnel training. Written as a narrative summary with the full SOP set delivered as a separate companion document.

04

Financial Model

Five-year financial projections including startup costs, operating expenses, revenue by product category, cash flow, and break-even analysis. Built in Excel with a summary narrative formatted for the licence application package.

05

Market Analysis

Canadian cannabis market sizing, provincial regulatory environment, competitive landscape, and target customer segment analysis. Data sourced from Health Canada cannabis market data reports and provincial regulatory publications.

06

Key Personnel

Biographies and regulatory role descriptions for the Master Grower, Head of Security, Quality Assurance Person, and other key personnel as required by the licence category. Formatted to demonstrate compliance with Health Canada personnel requirements.
